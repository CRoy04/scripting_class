What To Do

If you’re travelling in the area and are looking to checkout surrounding attractions around southern Alberta, Lethbridge is the place to stay. Being right in the middle of two national Parks: Banff and Waterton, which are each roughly only 2-3 hours away. If you’re looking to venture in and around city limits, Lethbridge is surrounded by provincial parks such as Writing on Stone, Indian Battle, Pavan, and Park Lake. Where you will find many hiking trails and camping spots.

We also have many cultural and historical sites around town such as Nikka Yuko Japanese Garden, Fort-Whoop Up, and the Galt Museum, where you can learn more about the city’s background. If you’re staying in the city; the famous Rocky Mountain Turf Club is a must-do experience. Home to an abundance of entertainment like Bully’s Casino and Bar, venues for events, and of course, horse racing. The club has single-handedly made a significant impact on the economy of Southern Alberta, not only though horse racing and gambling, but by impacting hay and feed operators, hotels, and service stations around the area.
