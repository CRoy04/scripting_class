Lethbridge is historically known for being oil, coal, and agriculture based. Over the years Lethbridge has moved away from coal and is now mainly oil and agriculture based. Although, an increasing number of diverse businesses, which are anchored to the community, have been thriving for years.

A few if the largest companies in the city include Lethbridge Industries LTD, Big D Enterprises, and Big Rock Plumbing and Heating. Lethbridge is also home to major oil and gas companies like JBA Petroleum Services Ltd, and Gas King Oil Co Ltd. Currently, the city is one of the most affordable in Canada, providing jobs for over 85, 000 people who travel to and in the city within a 100-kilometre radius.
