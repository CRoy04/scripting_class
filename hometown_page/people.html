Culture

During the late 1800s, Lethbridge was once populated by several First Nations Tribes at various times. Now, Lethbridge has a population of over 100,000, with a diverse range of culture throughout the city.

According to www.lethbridgenews.com, today the people of Lethbridge mainly hold origins of English (22.9%), German (19.2%), Scottish (17.3%), Irish (13.6%), and Canadian (13.4%). Overall, Lethbridge is made up of diverse, hardworking, friendly people, who are not afraid to stop and give you a hand when your car breaks down on the side of the road.
