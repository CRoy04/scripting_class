Location

Located on the prairies in the southern region of Alberta, approximately only 100 kilometers away from the US border. Lethbridge is the fourth largest city in Alberta after Calgary, Edmonton, and Red Deer. Being surrounded by open, lush farmland, gives a feeling of freedom and peace.
